# Past Exam — Corporate Finance (sample)

1. A project costs $1,000 today and returns $1,200 in one year. At a 10% discount rate,
   what is its NPV?  **Answer: $90.91.**

2. Which statement about the internal rate of return (IRR) is true?
   a) It is the discount rate that makes NPV zero
   b) It is always greater than the discount rate
   c) It ignores the time value of money
   d) It equals the payback period
   **Answer: (a).**

3. In one sentence, explain the time value of money.
   **Answer: a dollar today is worth more than a dollar in the future because it can be
   invested to earn a return.**
